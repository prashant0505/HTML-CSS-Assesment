About us

A Word About us

FitStop is a private gym based in Indore, India. After spending 6 years as a personal trainer, Prashant Patidar founded the gym so that he could continue to help people transform their lives and achieve their health and fitness goals. Since opening in September 2020, FitStop has helped hundreds of people change their lives.
